Welcome to CleanCity

CleanCity empowers citizens to report waste issues in their neighborhood quickly and effectively. Using this app, you can submit photos of waste, assign locations, and help keep your city clean and safe.

  • 📍 Report issues with photos and locations
  • 🧾 View and manage your submitted reports
  • 📊 Monitor cleanliness status area-wise
  • ✅ No login required, all data is local to your browser

Get started by navigating from the menu. Together, we build a cleaner community!
